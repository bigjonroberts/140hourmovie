Performance Art on a Minuscule Scale

Inspired by an insipid question
Leading to a ridiculous challenge
... moderately entertaining people around the world.

Here’s the deal. One movie, four hours of sleep per night, up to seven consecutive days, one guy, lots of money, charity, endless entertainment, tons of phrases separated by commas, etc. You get the idea. I’ll spend 140 hours watching a movie of your choice on repeat, and you’ll pay me to do it.

Is this an art project? A way for a dude to make some money to pay for college? An indirect donation to the San Antonio Texas Habitat for Humanity? An indirect donation to a charity of your choosing? An attempt at internet fame? An overpriced scam? Too many questions? It’s all of the above. Except for the scam one. This is not a scam. It’s a non bamboozle independent project where I promise to spend up to 7 days watching the same movie (or TV show), 20/7. The money you donate will be dispersed to charities upon the full funding of each day of the challenge, and I will not receive a dime until I have successfully completed the challenge. If I fail this challenge, I will only be able to keep $1,500 for all my hard work, and the remainder will go to the selected charities.
